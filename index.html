A bit about me

We're all after the same thing: clean, crisp copy that nails what we do and how we do it.

But platitudes aside, the vast majority of what we read on the web's plain and formulaic. And today in the Digital Age, boring copy → bouncing.

At your service, I, Tom di Mino, aim to absorb your personas—to unleash and augment them in a way that’ll excite you, your target audience, and (even future) search engines.

I'm ready for anything, in any industry. Drop me an email, or buzz my cell, and let's get creative together!
